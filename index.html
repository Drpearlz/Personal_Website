About me

My name is Pardeep Rathore, and I am software developer
I moved to Canada in 2016 as a permanent resident. Before moving here, I did MBA(Master of Business Administration) and bachelor in BCA(Bachelor of Computer Application) from Punjab Technical University, India. Due to international education background, I struggled to get a job. Therefore, I decided to go back to school in 2017. I have been working part-time/intership/coop jobs while doing Bachelor of Science Degree at UBC, and I am finishing my last two courses in April 2021. My major is Computer Sciecne and my minor is Data Science

I have two years development experience, and currently working as a part time web developer at Csek Creative Inc. in Kelowna. Last year, I worked as a coop software developer at SQM Group Inc for a year in Vernon. I have working experience in the following technologies:

Front-end:
HTML5, CSS/SCSS, JavaScript(Vanilla), Bootstrap, Material UI
Back-end:
C#(.Net Core Framwork, Entity core framework), Java, SQL Server, Php(CakePhp), MySQL

Currently, I am focusing on learning front-end framework angular and react, and backend framework spring boot. I am comfortable building responsive websites, and I have developed an android app as well.
